My Services

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy aids personal growth, offering support to explore and address challenges. Our therapists guide you in developing coping skills and fostering lasting change, potentially involving loved ones for enhanced understanding and collaboration.

Clinical Supervision

Recognizing the critical need for more therapists of color, Tacarra supervises therapists-in-training and emphasizing NASW standards. This includes guiding their application of social work theories and ethical principles.

Clinical Consultation

Clinical consultation is provided to other mental health and clinical social workers seeking feedback and assistance with growth, professional development, or consultation with difficult client cases.

Family Counseling

Navigate family challenges with compassionate guidance. Our experienced therapists help families improve communication, resolve conflicts, and build stronger, more fulfilling relationships.

About Me

With over 20 years in social services, Tacarra founded Ingram Counseling & Consulting to destigmatize mental health. She prioritizes a comfortable and non-judgmental environment where clients can explore concerns and develop strategies for positive change. Tacarra offers virtual counseling and supervision in North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia, meeting clients where they are.

Our Philosophy

We are a minority, and women owned mental health private practice based in North Carolina, serving North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia. Established in 2021, Ingram Counseling & Consulting, PLLC was created out of a passion for helping others find peace in a world full of chaos.

—

Our practice prides itself on serving communities where mental health treatment is not the norm. Many of our clients are seeking mental health treatment for the first time. Services are offered virtually to allow easy access to the services our clients need and desire.
